## Project

This is a thesis research project: a fictional Vejle Kommune (Danish municipality) site in Umbraco 17 used as a Demo vehicle for a catalogue of architectural Patterns for AI-in-CMS. **Before doing anything substantive, read `CONTEXT.md` and the ADRs in `docs/adr/`** — the design has been resolved through grilling and those documents are load-bearing. In particular: 7 Patterns across 8 build phases, with Phase 0+1 (content model + Schema enrichment) as the first deliverable.
